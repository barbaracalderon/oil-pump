The Oil & Gas Data Panel

Our Oil & Gas Data Panel provides real-time monitoring and analysis of critical metrics within the oil extraction industry. Integrated with cutting-edge technologies, it offers actionable insights into operational data for informed decision-making.

The dashboard aggregates data from sensors deployed across extraction facilities, including pressure, fluid characteristics, and material conditions. This data is pivotal for detecting anomalies, predicting maintenance needs, and optimizing operational efficiency.

Technical Details

  • Data Collection: Real-time data from sensors
  • API Backend: Developed in Go, integrated with PostgreSQL
  • Frontend: Angular-based dashboard for data visualization
  • Deployment: Containerized using Docker for scalability

The Oil & Gas Data Panel represents a significant advancement in the industry, enabling stakeholders to monitor operations effectively and improve decision-making processes. With intuitive visualizations and predictive analytics, the dashboard empowers users to enhance productivity and minimize downtime.
